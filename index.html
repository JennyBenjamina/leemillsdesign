About Alt ShotDesign

At Alternate Shot Marketing, we combine golf, technology, data science, and marketing expertise to elevate your club's presence and enhance member experiences. We offer strategies that delight members and add professional polish to your club, from boosting event participation to member communication.

Alt Shot Marketing boasts a global client base. It has set the standard for design in golf as its clients collaborate with the highest caliber of creatives and ambassadors.
